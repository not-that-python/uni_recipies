📖 How to add a recipe (click to expand)
  1. Fill in recipe details - Name, description, cooking time, cost level, and step-by-step instructions
  2. Add ingredients one by one - Type the ingredient name (e.g., "Egg") and quantity (e.g., "2 large"), then click "+ Add" or press Enter
  3. Keep adding until all ingredients are listed
  4. Click "Submit Recipe" - Your recipe will be saved and available immediately

💡 Tip: Ingredient names are automatically capitalized. If an ingredient already exists in our database, it will be reused!
